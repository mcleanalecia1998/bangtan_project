BTS 💜 ARMY

BTS, Bangtan Sonyeondan (Korean: “Bulletproof Boy Scouts” or “Bangtan Boys”) South Korean K-pop (Korean pop music) band that shot to international stardom in the late 2010s. The group debuted on June 12, 2013.
BTS had a devoted following ARMY which stands for “Adorable Representative MC for Youth”.
The band broke numerous records on YouTube—namely, for the most views of a music video within 24 hours of its release, for such singles as “Boy with Luv” (2019), “Dynamite” (2020), and “Butter” (2021). This year marks their 10 year anniversary. The group is currently on hiatus but the seven individual members has been keeping the fandom busy solo work before their mandatory military service.
